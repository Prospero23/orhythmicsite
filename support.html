FAQ

Adding & Removing Nodes

  • Q: How do I add a new node?
    A: Tap the “+” button.
  • Q: How can I delete a node?
    A: Select its parent and hit the "-" button.

Combining Nodes

  • Q: What does “Combine” do?
    A: Merges two adjacent nodes into a single tuplet.
  • Q: Can I undo a combine?
    A: Yes—once the merged nodes don't have children, they split.

Editing Measures

  • Q: How do I add a new measure?
    A: Tap "Edit Sequence", then press "Add Measure".
  • Q: How do I remove a measure?
    A: Tap "Edit Sequence", then press "Delete Measure".
  • Q: How do I change a measure's time signature?
    A: Tap the blue time signature that is beside "Edit Sequence" and change it as needed.

Troubleshooting

  • Q: Why can't I edit the notation on screen?
    A: Enter the edit screen by making sure the metronome is paused and pressing the pencil icon that appears.
  • Q: Why did ORhythmic stop playing audio?
    A: Sometimes permissions get weird after opening another app. Restarting the app should fix this.
